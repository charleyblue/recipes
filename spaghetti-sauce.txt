Ingedients:
2 cans of tomatoes; whole, chopped, or puree
2 tbsp tomato paste
1 med onion chopped
4 cloves garlic chopped
6 large mushrooms sliced
1 red bell pepper chopped
1 green bell pepper chopped
2-4 tbsp olive oil
seasoning to taste: oregano, thyme, basil, salt, pepper, coriander seed, cayenne
pepper, cumin, Etc.
1 tsp sugar

1. Put tomatoes in a large pot and bring to a simmer. While waiting:
2. Chop vegetables to 3/4" chuncks.
3. Begin to sautee the onions in 1 tbsp olive oil 5 mins.
4. add bell peppers, seasoning and cook until onions begin to caramelize, 10 mins.
5. Add sauteed onions and bell peppers to tomato sauce.
6. Sautee the mushrooms until lightly browned 10 mins. Add to sauce.
7. Add chopped garlic remaining seasoning and sugar to sauce.
8. Simmer on low heat for 1 -2 hours.
9. Boil water for pasta, cook to desired texture. Drain
10. Heat remaining oil in same sautee pan used for vegies on med heat.
11. place a serving of pasta in sautee pan, spoon sauce over pasta, and mix and
heat for 1 min.
12. Serve with grated cheese and enjoy!
A fresh salad makes a good side to this dish.
Note: can add browned ground beef, meatballs, etc to this sauce.
